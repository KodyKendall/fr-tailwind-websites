The Importance of Safety in Elderly Bathroom Remodels

Did you know that falls in the bathroom, particularly getting in and out of bathtubs, are a major cause of injury for older adults? This is why our expert contractor, Alex Castro, focuses on creating safer bathroom environments for our elderly clients.

Key Safety Features in Our Recent Remodel for Teri

  • Removal of the bathtub to eliminate a major fall hazard
  • Installation of multiple grab bars, including a 20-inch bar and additional support near the toilet
  • Reinforced walls to ensure grab bars are securely anchored

Going Above and Beyond in Bathroom Remodeling

At 2 Tile Bath, Kitchen, and Tile Remodels, we don't believe in cutting corners. Our approach to Utah County bathroom remodeling involves:

  1. Thorough demolition and reframing to reinforce walls for grab bar installation
  2. Using solid anchoring techniques instead of less reliable methods
  3. Relocating light switches for better accessibility
  4. Installing bath fans for improved ventilation

Alex Castro, our dedicated contractor, often goes above and beyond, even when it means extra effort without additional charges. Why? Because we prioritize our customers' safety and satisfaction above all else.
