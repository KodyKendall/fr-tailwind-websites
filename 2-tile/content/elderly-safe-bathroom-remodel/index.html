The Importance of Safety in Elderly Bathroom Remodels

Did you know that falls in the bathroom, particularly getting in and out of bathtubs, are a major cause of injury for older adults? This is why I focus on creating safer bathroom environments for our elderly clients. It's not just about aesthetics; it's about peace of mind.

Key Safety Features in Our Recent Remodel for Teri

  • I removed the bathtub to eliminate a major fall hazard
  • I installed multiple grab bars, including a 20-inch bar and additional support near the toilet
  • I reinforced the walls to ensure grab bars are securely anchored

Going Above and Beyond in Bathroom Remodeling

At 2 Tile Bath, Kitchen, and Tile Remodels, I don't believe in cutting corners. My approach to Utah County bathroom remodeling involves:

  1. Thorough demolition and reframing to reinforce walls for grab bar installation
  2. Using solid anchoring techniques instead of less reliable methods
  3. Relocating light switches for better accessibility
  4. Installing bath fans for improved ventilation

I often go above and beyond, even when it means extra effort without additional charges. Why? Because I prioritize my customers' safety and satisfaction above all else. It's not just a job for me; it's a commitment to improving lives.
